2.3.3.3.132. NXmpes_liquid¶

Status:

application definition (contribution), extends NXmpes

Description:

This is the application definition for multidimensional photoelectron spectrosco ...

This is the application definition for multidimensional photoelectron spectroscopy on liquids. It extends the NXmpes application definition by metadata for characterizing liquidjet composition and geometry.

Symbols:

No symbol table

Groups cited:

NXentry, NXenvironment, NXinstrument, NXsample_component, NXsample, NXtransformations

Structure:

ENTRY: (required) NXentry ⤆

Obligatory value: NXmpes_liquid

@version: (required) NX_CHAR ⤆

INSTRUMENT: (required) NXinstrument ⤆

instrument_orientation: (optional) NXtransformations

Transformations describing base geometry in the experiment. ...

Transformations describing base geometry in the experiment. Usually defaults to the beam in z direction and gravity in -y direction.

analyzer_optical_axis: (recommended) NX_NUMBER ⤆

The coordinate system frame of the analyzer. ...

The coordinate system frame of the analyzer. If no direction attribute is specified, the optical axis of the analyzer follows the positive z-direction.

@gravity: (required) NX_CHAR

Direction of gravity in the analyzer coordinate system.

@beam_direction: (required) NX_CHAR

Direction of the photon beam in the analyzer coordinate system.

@direction: (required) NX_CHAR

The direction of the analyzer optical axis. ...

The direction of the analyzer optical axis. If this is not specified the optical axis points in the positive jet direction. It is recommended to always specify the axis direction explicitly (even for the default case).

SAMPLE: (required) NXsample ⤆

thickness: (recommended) NX_FLOAT {units=NX_LENGTH} ⤆

Thickness of the sample in normal direction to the surface that is probed, ...

Thickness of the sample in normal direction to the surface that is probed, e.g., diameter of capillary (round jets) or thickness of the leaf (flat jets).

flow_rate: (recommended) NX_FLOAT {units=NX_ANY}

Flow rate of the liquid into the chamber, i.e., ...

Flow rate of the liquid into the chamber, i.e., through the microjet capillary (or in case of a flat jet: both capillaries). Unit should be NX_VOLUME / NX_TIME.

shape: (required) NX_CHAR

Any of these values: round | flat

nozzle_material: (recommended) NX_CHAR

Material the microjet nozzle is made of ...

Material the microjet nozzle is made of (users are encouraged to expand on that in group history/sample_preparation).

depends_on: (recommended) NX_CHAR ⤆

Connects this liquidjet to its transformation chain.

temperature_env: (optional) NXenvironment ⤆

The temperature set for the liquid jet or the injection assembly. ...

The temperature set for the liquid jet or the injection assembly. Details may be added through instances of NXsensor/NXactuator on how this temperature value is obtained (whether a chiller unit was used, point of cooling, temperature reading etc.) and where this value would be expected (estimate for the jet assembly, the liquid prior to injection, or at the interaction point taking into account evaporative cooling etc.).

gas_pressure_env: (optional) NXenvironment ⤆

Pressure in the interaction chamber under operation conditions and actuato ...

Pressure in the interaction chamber under operation conditions and actuators/sensors controlling/measuring it.

liquidjet_orientation: (required) NXtransformations ⤆

Transformations describing the transformation chain of the liquid jet flow ...

Transformations describing the transformation chain of the liquid jet flow direction and optionally the flat jet normal. The vector of the major flow direction of the liquid jet from the point of injection into the chamber. Along the cylinder axis for single, cylindrical jets, and parallel to the leaf plane for flatjets.

jet_direction: (required) NX_NUMBER {units=NX_ANGLE} ⤆

The coordinate system frame of the liquid jet direction. ...

The coordinate system frame of the liquid jet direction. If no direction attribute is specified, the jet points in the positive z-direction.

@gravity: (recommended) NX_NUMBER

The direction of gravity in the coordinate system ...

The direction of gravity in the coordinate system of jet_direction.

@transformation_type: (required) NX_CHAR ⤆

Obligatory value: rotation

@direction: (recommended) NX_NUMBER

The direction of the liquid jet. ...

The direction of the liquid jet. If this is not specified the jet points in the positive jet direction. It is recommended to always specify the jet direction explicitly (even for the default case).

leaf_normal: (required) NX_NUMBER {units=NX_ANGLE} ⤆

Coordinate frame for the surface normal of the first leaf on the side of ...

Coordinate frame for the surface normal of the first leaf on the side of solvent #1. If no direction is specified, the surface leaf normal vector points in the positive z-direction. The value of this field is the angle between the analyzer optical axis and the leaf normal. If you only want to collect the angle between the leaf normal and the analyzer optical axis the depends_on attribute may be omitted. If a transformation chain is provided (with the depends_on attribute) it should depend on the analyzer optical axis or the jet direction depending on the use case.

@transformation_type: (required) NX_CHAR ⤆

Obligatory value: rotation

@direction: (recommended) NX_NUMBER

Direction of surface normal vector of the first leaf on the side of so ...

Direction of surface normal vector of the first leaf on the side of solvent #1. If this is not specified it defaults to the positive z-direction which should be parallel to the detection direction. It is recommended to always specify the leaf normal direction explicitly (even for the default case).

solventSOLVENT: (required) NXsample_component ⤆

Solvent substance of the liquid jet. ...

Solvent substance of the liquid jet. For flat jets, this group may appear more than once. If a mixture of solvents is used, the one with the highest mol fraction should be designated solvent, and the other(s) solute(s) (see solute group).

description: (recommended) NX_CHAR ⤆

name: (required) NX_CHAR ⤆

chemical_formula: (required) NX_CHAR ⤆

Molecular or structural formula of the solvent.

chem_id_cas: (recommended) NX_CHAR

ph_value: (optional) NX_FLOAT {units=NX_UNITLESS}

Measured pH value of the solution.

ph_adjustment: (optional) NX_BOOLEAN

Whether or not agents for pH adjustment were added to the solution. ...

Whether or not agents for pH adjustment were added to the solution. In this case, users are encouraged to expand on that in the history group or add a corresponding solute group.

mol_fraction: (optional) NX_FLOAT {units=NX_ANY}

Molarity of the solvent in the solution. ...

Molarity of the solvent in the solution. Unit should be 1 / NX_VOLUME.

soluteSOLUTE: (required) NXsample_component ⤆

Solute substance solved in a given solvent. ...

Solute substance solved in a given solvent. This also accounts for multiple solutes if more than one is given.

description: (recommended) NX_CHAR ⤆

name: (required) NX_CHAR ⤆

chemical_formula: (required) NX_CHAR ⤆

Molecular or structural formula of the solute.

chem_id_cas: (recommended) NX_CHAR

mol_fraction: (recommended) NX_FLOAT {units=NX_ANY}

Molarity of the solute in the solution. ...

Molarity of the solute in the solution. Unit should be 1 / NX_VOLUME

solvent: (required) NX_CHAR

Specifies the parent solvent entry of the solution.
