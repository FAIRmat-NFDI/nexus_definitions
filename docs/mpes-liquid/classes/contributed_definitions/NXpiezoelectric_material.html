2.3.3.3.139. NXpiezoelectric_material¶

Status:

base class (contribution), extends NXcomponent

Description:

Description and properties of the piezoelectric actuator materials. ...

Description and properties of the piezoelectric actuator materials. The piezoelectric actuator is usually composed of polycrystalline solids and attached at the head of the tip or cantilever. The material deforms when the external electric field is applied.
