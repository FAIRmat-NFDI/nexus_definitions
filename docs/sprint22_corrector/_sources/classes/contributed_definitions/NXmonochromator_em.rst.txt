==================
NXmonochromator_em
==================

**Status**:

  base class, extends :ref:`NXcomponent_em`

**Description**:

  .. collapse:: Base class for improving energy resolution in an electron microscope ...

      Base class for improving energy resolution in an electron microscope
      or reducing chromatic aberration.

      Examples are Wien, $\textalpha$-, or $\Omega$- energy filter or
      `cc corrector like <https://www.ceos-gmbh.de/en/basics/cc-corrector>`_
