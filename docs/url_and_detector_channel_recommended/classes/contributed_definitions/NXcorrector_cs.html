Base class for a corrector reducing (spherical) aberrations in electron microscopy.

Different technology partners use different naming schemes and models for quantifying the aberration coefficients.

The corrector in an electron microscope is composed of multiple lenses and multipole stigmators with details specific for the technology partner and microscope. Many of their technical details is proprietary knowledge.

If functionalities for correcting multiple aberrations are included in one NXcomponent like it is reported here use multiple groups:

  • NXcorrector_cs for spherical aberration

  • NXmonochromator for energy filtering or chromatic aberration

  • corrector_ax in NXem for axial astigmatism correction
